It is completely unexpected. Sometimes it can be pleasant, a great and sudden explosion of joy. Sometimes it can be a deep wave of sorrow, overwhelming and immediate. Sometimes it is a terrible, ghastly fright, a shock from waking from a nightmare. Rarely it is so confounding, so intensely against our expectations, that we stand there, dumbfounded, awestruck, at a loss for words or thoughts of any kind. In any case it always comes when you least expect it.
